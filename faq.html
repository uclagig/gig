FAQs

What is GIG?
GIG is a completely free professional network for university students. We collect student resumes and put them in the hands of companies, recruiters, and alumni. We are currently focused on UCLA Computer Science and Computer Science and Engineering students.
How do I send in my resume?
Sign up with your name and UCLA email address here. Shortly after, we will send you an email containing a link to drop off your resume.
Who will see my resume?
The founders, Rakshit Garg and Rohan Kapoor, will have access to your resume for sending purposes only. Resumes will be sent out to trusted recruiters, companies, and UCLA alumni.
How do I send in an updated resume?
Contact us here. We'll have your resume replaced in no time.
How do I remove my resume from GIG?
Contact us here and we will remove it. We appreciate any feedback so we can better improve our services and hopefully alleviate some of the stress of professional networking.
What do I do if I am an interested recruiter?
Head on over to our recruitment page. We ask that you fill out a form about yourself and the company you are affiliated with. We will get in contact with your shortly after.
Do I need to pay for any services?
Nope! As students ourselves, we want to make this process as easy as possible.
As a UCLA alumnus, how do I get involved?
If you are looking to recruit talented students, head on over to our recruitment page. If not and you want to work with us to better improve our services, feel free to contact us here.
Who can I contact if I have a question, comment, or concern?
Head on over here to our contact page. You can also contact the founders, Rohan Kapoor and Rakshit Garg.
